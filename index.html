About the Poet

I am Iron Heist (John Colt), a poet and philosopher weaving words from the depths of my soul. My journey with poetry began as a way to process the complexities of love, loss, and the human experience. Each verse I write is a reflection of my heart—a tapestry of emotions that I hope resonates with those who feel deeply.

When I'm not writing, you can find me lost in thought by the ocean, sketching the stars, or sharing my musings on social media. Thank you for joining me on this poetic journey.
